About Us

David Todic

I'm David Todic, an 18-year-old aspiring copywriter from Austria, fueled by ambition and driven by a competitive mindset, discipline, a love for football, and a dedication to fitness.


With a passion for crafting compelling copy that captivates audiences and drives results, I bring a strategic approach to my work, much like a game of chess. Every word is carefully chosen to create powerful messages that resonate with the target audience.


What sets me apart is my relentless determination and unwavering discipline. I thrive on challenges and always push myself to exceed expectations. I believe that success is born out of hard work, perseverance, and a commitment to continuous learning.


In addition to my professional pursuits, I prioritize my physical well-being through regular workouts. I understand the importance of maintaining a healthy body and mind, as it directly influences my creativity and productivity.


If you're looking for a dedicated and driven copywriter to elevate your brand and bring your business forward with a competitive edge, I'm here to collaborate and help you achieve your goals.


Let's connect and embark on a journey towards success together.

Uros Nisavic

I'm Uros Nisavic, a 20-year-old copywriter from Austria with a penchant for chess, fitness, and all-you-can-eat buffets.


Just like in a challenging game of chess, I believe that every word in copywriting needs to be strategic and purposeful. With over a year of experience in crafting compelling copy for local restaurants and gyms, I've honed my skills to create content that strikes the perfect balance between persuasion and authenticity.


Now, I'm bringing my proven track record to the digital world. I am dedicated to helping businesses amplify their message, engage their audience, and drive action - all through the power of words!


When I'm not writing or analyzing chess strategies, you'll find me at the gym, trying out a new all-you-can-eat restaurant, or hunting for obscure english words like "succinct". This balance keeps my creativity flowing and my perspective fresh, enriching my work and ensuring your brand stands out.


Ready to take your brand narrative to the next level? Let's connect and create something great!
